Lojix

Lojix is a purpose-built system to provide resource planning for contract management, workforce collaboration, timesheets, travel and expense reporting, accounting, budgeting and forecasting, billing, and financials with cost pool calculations, in a single integrated and affordable system.

Lojix is designed to provide a flexible ERP system available to government contractors. It can be configured to function the way you execute your business while supporting continuous process improvement. Our system is constantly evolving to offer innovation and growth.

Lojix integrates and centrally stores financial, contract management, and human resources information. All of your employees can view and share accurate data in real time, leading to increased collaboration and productivity across your business.
