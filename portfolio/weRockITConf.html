WeRockIT Conf

WeRockIT Conf is an annual two day technology conference located in Huntsville, AL. They have a mission to highlight and grow diverse voices in technology.

The conference is organized by members of Women Who Code Huntsville. WWCode Huntsville is a group of developers,engineers, and business professionals in the Huntsville area. WeRockIT Conf will include a mix of panel discussions, keynotes, speakers, and hands-on workshops.
