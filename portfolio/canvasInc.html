Canvas, Inc

Canvas is a woman owned small business founded in 2007 located in Huntsville, AL. Canvas, Inc's vision is to be recognized by their customers, business partners, and community for their achieving the art of the possible. Canvas, Inc is committed to providing a family-oriented corporate culture and promotion work/life balance. They consider this an integral part of being one of the best places to work in Huntsville.

Canvas, Inc provides services to a large, diverse group of customers in the areas of Test and ; Engineering Services (Aerospace, Radar, Software and Missile Engineering); Program Management (Financial, Cost, Accounting, and EVMS); Acquisition Management; and Product Integration. They have a highly-advanced toolbox of skill-sets and support numerous test and mission execution events, program improvements, strategy panels and boards, fund tracking system developments, and product fabrication for the Warfighter.
